Configure Slack Web-hooks

Guide to using WEB-HOOKS on SLACK

  1. First, log in to Slack.com via your browser.

  1. This is the main interface of Slack after successful login.

  1. Next, access Incoming WebHooks and click Add to Slack.

  1. When clicked, you’ll be prompted to Create Another Workspace. Click on it.

  2. This is the interface that appears after clicking Create Another Workspace. Enter the name aws-lambda-labs and then select Following.

  1. Next, enter your name and click Following.

  1. Skip this step if you are working alone.

  1. Done.
